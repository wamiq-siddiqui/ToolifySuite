Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how ToolifySuite (“we”, “us”, or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website toolifysuite.netlify.app and use our online tools.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences, so that your experience can be smoother and more useful.

2. How we use cookies

ToolifySuite focuses on fast, privacy-friendly tools. We aim to use a minimal and privacy-respecting set of cookies and similar technologies. Depending on how you use the site, we may use cookies for:

  • Remembering basic preferences (such as theme or previously used settings).
  • Improving performance and stability of the site.
  • Measuring anonymous usage statistics to understand which tools are used the most.
  • Displaying advertising or affiliate content (if enabled in the future).

3. Types of cookies we may use

a) Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function correctly. Without them, core features such as basic navigation, security, or saving simple tool preferences may not work. You cannot disable these cookies through our site.

b) Preference (functional) cookies

These cookies remember your choices, such as preferred tool options or interface settings.

c) Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use ToolifySuite. Data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

d) Third-party cookies

Some services may set their own cookies (analytics, ads, embedded content).

4. Cookies used by our tools

Many tools run locally inside your browser. Some may use local/session storage to:

  • Remember last used settings
  • Prevent abuse or limit repeated requests
  • Store temporary tool state

5. How to control cookies

Browsers allow full cookie control. You can block, delete, or restrict cookies easily.

  • View stored cookies
  • Delete cookies
  • Block cookies for specific websites
  • Block third-party cookies or all cookies

6. Managing cookies in browsers

Cookie settings differ across Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari, Opera.

7. Do we use cookies for advertising?

ToolifySuite currently focuses on tools. Ads may use cookies in the future.

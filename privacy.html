Privacy Policy

ToolifySuite is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used and the rights you have when using our tools and website.

Information We Collect

ToolifySuite does not collect personal data such as names, emails or uploaded files. All tools are fully client-side and run directly in your browser. No processing occurs on external servers.

Usage Data

We may use anonymous analytics to understand general usage trends such as page views or tool popularity. This data cannot identify individual users.

Cookies

ToolifySuite uses minimal cookies only when required for website functionality. No tracking, advertising or third-party behavioral cookies are used.

Third-Party Services

Our site may display third-party ads (such as Google AdSense in the future). These services may collect data according to their own policies. You can review their privacy terms separately.

Data Security

All tools are designed to run locally in your browser, meaning your data stays on your device. We do not store, sell or share your information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the website confirms your acceptance of the updated terms.
